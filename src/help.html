Help & Financial Tips

Learn how to use Finomic and improve your financial habits

About Finomic

Your personal financial assistant designed to help you track, analyze, and optimize your finances.

Finomic makes it easy to monitor your income and expenses, understand spending patterns, and make informed financial decisions. All your data is stored locally in your browser for privacy and security.

Key Features

Dashboard Overview

Get a quick snapshot of your total balance, income, and expenses. Track your financial health at a glance.

Transaction Management

Add, view, and filter all your transactions. Categorize income and expenses for better organization.

Category Insights

Analyze spending patterns by category. Identify where your money goes and optimize your budget.

Monthly Reports

Visualize financial trends with charts and graphs. Compare income vs expenses over time.

Financial Tips & Best Practices
💰

50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% of income to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment.

🎯

Set Clear Goals

Define specific financial goals like emergency fund (3-6 months expenses), retirement savings, or major purchases.

📈

Track Every Expense

Monitor all spending, no matter how small. Small expenses add up and can reveal spending patterns.

🛡️

Build Emergency Fund

Aim to save 3-6 months of living expenses for unexpected situations like job loss or medical emergencies.

💡

Automate Savings

Set up automatic transfers to savings accounts right after payday. Pay yourself first.

📖

Review Monthly

Review your finances monthly to identify trends, adjust budgets, and celebrate progress.

How to Use Finomic

1
Add Your Transactions

Go to the Transactions page and click "Add Transaction". Enter the amount, category, description, and date.

2
Monitor Your Dashboard

Check the Dashboard regularly to see your total balance, income, and expenses.

3
Analyze by Category

Visit the Categories page to see where your money is going. Identify top spending categories.

4
Review Monthly Reports

Use the Reports page to track trends over time. Compare monthly income vs expenses.
